hello!

Thanks for visiting. My name is Melanie Tabroff, and I'm a product designer based in NYC. I’ve spent the past 2 years working as a UX/UI designer and researcher at various startups. Most recently I worked at TrialSpark, a biotech company using technology to run end-to-end clinical trials. I love learning about new industries and collaborating with a team to solve interesting problems.

Quick facts

  • I’m a virgo.
  • My academic background is in cognitive psychology and biology, which has largely influenced my approach to design: I start by apply systems thinking (eg understand the core elements of a product, how they interact) and dive deep into understanding its users (how do they think, what drives them to behave the way the do, what do they want to accomplish by using the product)
  • I’m an avid runner and outdoor health enthusiast. (You can find me on Strava!)
  • I like to draw people people for fun.
  • Bill Burnett & Dave Evan’s book Designing Your Life inspired me to pursue a career in design.
  • Design advice that has really resonated with me comes from Intercome’s blog: a product designer’s job is to ship product, not design.
